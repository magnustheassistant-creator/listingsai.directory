Tools That Actually Save You Money

AppSumo ⭐ Top pick

Not yet affiliated

Lifetime software deals — pay once, use forever. Massive catalog, 60-day refund window on every purchase. The flagship "save money" play. Some vendors disappear, so vet before buying expensive deals.

Best for: Anyone paying monthly for software they use long-term. Watch out: Some deals are duds, some vendors disappear after the deal. Read reviews before buying.

Hostinger

Not yet affiliated

Budget web hosting from $2.99/mo (48-month commit). Free domain first year on Premium+. LiteSpeed servers, NVMe storage. Real performance, not oversold-budget-tier junk.

Watch out: Renewal prices jump. Read the renewal cost, not the intro. Chat-only support, no phone.

Cloudways

Not yet affiliated

Managed cloud hosting (DigitalOcean, AWS, GCP, Linode under the hood). From $14/mo. Better performance than shared hosting but pricier. For sites that need real speed.

Best for: Sites with traffic, ecommerce stores, agencies. Steeper learning curve than Hostinger.

Kinsta

Not yet affiliated

Premium managed WordPress hosting on Google Cloud. $35-700/mo. Excellent support, real performance. PayPal only — confirm your country works.

Best for: Serious WordPress sites where performance and support matter. Overkill for hobby projects.

Wise (TransferWise)

Not yet affiliated

Send money between countries at the real exchange rate (no hidden bank markups). Critical if you earn from US/UK clients and live in SEA. Saves hundreds vs PayPal or banks.

Best for: Anyone receiving international payments. Free to open, 0.4-1.5% fees vs 3-7% at banks.

NordVPN

Not yet affiliated

Privacy + access geo-locked deals (sometimes flights, SaaS, and digital products are cheaper in different regions). $3-13/mo depending on plan length. Audited no-logs policy.

Best for: Privacy + occasional geo-arbitrage. Don't use for anything sketchy.

Surfshark

Not yet affiliated

Budget VPN alternative to NordVPN. $2-13/mo. Unlimited devices (vs Nord's 6). Good if you have a lot of devices to cover.

Proton (VPN + Mail + Drive)

Not yet affiliated

Privacy-focused suite from the makers of ProtonMail. Free VPN tier. Paid from $4-30/mo for VPN + Mail + Drive + Calendar bundle. Swiss-based, audited.

Dealify

Not yet affiliated

AppSumo alternative. Smaller catalog. ~30% commission if we sign up. Worth checking for second-source deals.

What we look for in Save Money tools

Real free plans — not "free" with aggressive upsells. We verify free tiers are actually functional before recommending them. We test the refund policy, check if the company is stable, and skip anything with too many asterisks.
