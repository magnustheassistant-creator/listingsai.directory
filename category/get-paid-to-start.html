Get Paid to Start

Free Tools, Sign-up Bonuses, and Platforms That Pay You to Start

The infrastructure you need before you can earn anything online. None of these are currently affiliate-supported — but the free tiers are real, and the sign-up bonuses are honest. We flag the ones that are just marketing tricks.

INFO Informational — not yet affiliated

Payoneer ⭐ Set up first

Universal Payout

Universal payout rail for 200+ countries. Works in SEA, Latin America, Africa, and other underserved regions. Gives you a US/EU virtual bank account, which unlocks programs that wouldn't otherwise pay you. Free to sign up.

Why first: Without Payoneer, you can't get paid by Fiverr, Upwork, Amazon Associates, or most affiliate networks in non-US countries. Do this before anything else.

Fiverr (to EARN from)

Earn From Day 1

Sell a service starting at $5 — design, writing, video editing, voice-over, virtual assistant work. You keep 80% of every sale (Fiverr takes 20%). The fastest path to your first $100 online if you have a marketable skill.

Watch out: Race-to-the-bottom pricing in saturated categories. Stand out by being specific about what you offer.

ClickFunnels Free Trial

Affiliate · 14 Days

Get 14 days of ClickFunnels free. No credit card required. Build your first funnel before deciding whether to pay. This is the only affiliate-supported tool on this page — if you sign up through our link we may earn a commission.

Systeme.io Free Plan

Not yet affiliated

Free plan: 2,000 contacts, full funnels, courses, email marketing. No credit card required. The only catch is a small "made with Systeme.io" badge in the footer.

Best for: Building your first online business without paying for software.

Beehiiv Free Newsletter

Not yet affiliated

Free up to 2,500 subscribers. Built-in ad network once you hit 1,000 subs. Best newsletter platform for new creators. PayPal only — confirm your country works before committing.

Make.com Free Tier

Not yet affiliated

Free 1,000 operations/month. Enough to learn the platform and run small personal automations. Steeper learning curve than Zapier but more powerful. Will need a paid plan ($9-29/mo) once you start serving real clients.

Notion Free

Not yet affiliated

Free for personal use. Build second brains, content calendars, knowledge bases, and sellable templates. Solid foundation for any content or knowledge work.

Deel (global contractor payments)

Not yet affiliated

Get paid as a global contractor. Useful if you work remotely for international clients. Pairs well with Payoneer. ~$100+ per referral commission if we sign up.

Wise Multi-Currency Account

Not yet affiliated

Free to open. Multi-currency account, mid-market rate conversions, 0.4-1.5% fees. The best way to convert Payoneer USD/EUR payouts to your local currency.
