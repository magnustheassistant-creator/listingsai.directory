I'm All In (Funnel Builder Secrets Bundle)

Russell Brunson's signature premium upsell from the Funnel Builder Secrets webinar. If you decide ClickFunnels is your platform, this is the high-ticket bundle that gives you 12 months of the highest tier plus the Traffic Secrets framework, all for one payment instead of monthly.

What's actually in the bundle

  • 12 months of ClickFunnels Scale — the highest CF tier (~$2,487/yr if paid annually). Unlimited funnels, pages, contacts, follow-up emails, and the full feature set.
  • Traffic Secrets book + full course — Brunson's third book in the DotComSecrets / ExpertSecrets / TrafficSecrets trilogy, with the course walkthrough.
  • Traffic Swipe File — pre-built collection of proven ad copy, headlines, and funnel scripts you can deploy in your own funnels.
  • Conversation Domination LIVE community — private community for traffic and funnel questions, weekly Q&A, peer support.

The honest pros

  • Real savings vs buying separately. $1,970 vs ~$2,964 for 12 months of Scale + Traffic Secrets book. Saves ~$1,000+.
  • 12 months of runway. Plenty of time to build funnels and (hopefully) revenue before any renewal conversation.
  • Traffic Secrets is genuinely useful. The "Dream 100" + channel-by-channel breakdown is a real, repeatable system — not magic, but solid.
  • One ecosystem, one learning curve. CF Scale + Traffic Secrets + community in one place.
  • 30-day refund window (verify at checkout). You can exit cleanly if it's not for you.

The honest cons

  • Not for total beginners. If you haven't used ClickFunnels and aren't sure you want to build funnels, $1,970 is too much before validating the platform. Start with the free trial first.
  • The $9,349 "value" is anchor pricing. Real combined value is closer to $3,000-4,000. The discount is still real, just not 79%.
  • ClickFunnels is one platform. If your business model doesn't need funnels (portfolio, marketplace, app), this bundle doesn't make sense.
  • Year 2 is $2,487. Plan for renewal cost. By then you should have data on whether Scale is worth keeping.
  • High-energy teaching style. Watch a free FBS webinar first to gauge fit.

The math, simplified

Buying the bundle: $1,970 one-time. Includes 12mo CF Scale + Traffic Secrets + Swipe File + Community.

Buying separately over a year: $297/mo × 12 = $3,564 for CF Scale. Plus ~$300 for Traffic Secrets. Total: ~$3,864.

Savings: ~$1,894 over the year.

The verdict

If you've already decided on ClickFunnels: yes, it's a real deal. The savings vs paying monthly are clear, and Traffic Secrets is genuinely useful. If you're new to online business or undecided about ClickFunnels: no, start with the free trial first. The bundle is for people who've already validated the platform and want to commit.

ClickFunnels — the starting point

If you want to build funnels for a living, ClickFunnels is still the most proven option. 10+ years in business, 100,000+ users, the largest "make money online" community on the web.

What it is

All-in-one sales funnel builder — funnels + email + courses + checkout + affiliate management on one platform. The 14-day free trial requires no credit card.

What it costs

  • Basic — $97/mo (or $81/mo annual): 20 funnels, 5,000 contacts. Email is metered ($0.10/recipient above limits).
  • Pro — $197/mo (or $159/mo annual): Unlimited everything, 5 users, 25,000 contacts, unlimited email.
  • Scale — $297/mo (or $247/mo annual): Everything unlimited, 10 users, priority support.

Want 12 months of Scale for $1,970 instead of $2,964 monthly? See the I'm All In bundle above.

Honest pros

  • Ecosystem is unmatched. 10+ years of templates, training (FBS, Traffic Secrets, weekly webinars), community, integrations.
  • All-in-one that actually works. No need to stitch together 5 tools.
  • Pro and Scale plans have unlimited email. Real money saved at scale.
  • 14-day free trial, no credit card. You can actually test it before committing.

Honest cons

  • Basic plan email is metered. Costs add up if you have an active list.
  • Templates are showing their age. Some look like 2015-era internet marketing. You can build modern designs, but it takes more work.
  • Course hosting is locked to Scale plan ($297/mo). Big price jump for that one feature.
  • Pricing is high vs alternatives. Systeme.io starts at $27/mo (much cheaper), Moosend email is $9/mo. ClickFunnels is the premium option in the funnel-builder space.
