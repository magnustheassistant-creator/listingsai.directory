Systeme.io ⭐ Top Pick

60% Recurring for Life

Best for: Anyone building an online business — funnels, email lists, courses, and digital products on one platform. The free plan is genuinely usable (up to 2,000 contacts). The 60% recurring commission is the highest sustainable rate we've seen.

What it costs: Free plan (2,000 contacts), then $27/mo Startup, $47/mo Webinar, $97/mo Unlimited. Payouts via PayPal or wire — works in SEA.

Honest pros: All-in-one (no Zapier needed for basic stuff), generous free tier, real support, no transaction fees on the free plan.

Real cons: Templates look dated compared to Kajabi or ClickFunnels. Email deliverability is OK but not best-in-class — warm up your domain first.

Works globally? Yes. Instant approval. Pays PayPal and wire worldwide.

Make.com ⭐ Top Pick

35% Recurring

Best for: Anyone who wants to automate boring work without code — connecting apps, scraping data, sending notifications, building AI agents. Cleaner payout than Zapier (pays via Wise), and the AI-automation niche is hot right now.

What it costs: Free tier (1,000 ops/mo), Core $9/mo, Pro $16/mo, Teams $29/mo. Per-operation pricing is honest — you see what you use.

Honest pros: Visual builder is genuinely powerful. Payout via Wise is the cleanest SEA option we've seen. Lower competition than Zapier in the affiliate space.

Real cons: Steeper learning curve than Zapier. Free tier is too small for production use — you'll need a paid plan quickly.

Works globally? Yes. Approval via PartnerStack. Payouts to 180+ countries via Wise.

⭐ 4.8/5 Free tier Pays via Wise

Beehiiv

Up to 60% Recurring

Best for: Newsletter creators who want to monetize a growing list. The newsletter economy is booming and Beehiiv is far less saturated than ConvertKit for affiliates.

What it costs: Free up to 2,500 subs, Launch $49/mo, Scale $99/mo, Max $399/mo. Ad network lets you monetize even small lists.

Honest pros: Built by ex-Morning Brew people — they know newsletters. Built-in ad network is real income. Clean, modern editor.

Real cons: PayPal-only payouts (confirm in your country). Fewer integrations than ConvertKit. The 60% tier is only for first 12 months.

Works globally? Mostly yes, but check PayPal availability in your country before promoting.

⭐ 4.6/5 Free tier Ad network

Kartra

40% + 10% Tier 2

Best for: Course creators and coaches who need everything in one place — pages, email, video, checkout, helpdesk. Often overlooked vs ClickFunnels, so less competition in the affiliate space.

What it costs: Starter $99/mo, Silver $199/mo, Gold $499/mo, Platinum $999/mo. 14-day trial at $1.

Honest pros: 10% second-tier is rare and valuable. Genuinely all-in-one — fewer tools to integrate. Solid agency program if you build funnels for clients.

Real cons: Price jumps fast as you scale. UI feels older than Systeme.io. Not as beginner-friendly as Systeme.io.

Works globally? Yes. PayPal and wire payouts available.

GetResponse

40–60% Tiered

Best for: Email marketing with built-in webinars, automation, and e-commerce features. Lower-key than ConvertKit — less competition for affiliates.

What it costs: Free (up to 500 contacts), Email Marketing $19/mo, Marketing Automation $59/mo, Ecommerce $159/mo.

Honest pros: Mature platform (since 1998). Webinar feature is genuinely good. 90-day cookie is generous.

Real cons: Tiered commission means you earn less at the start. Interface feels dated in places.

Works globally? Yes. PartnerStack handles payouts, supports most countries.
