Systeme.io

Not yet affiliated

The most aggressive value play in the category. Free plan (2,000 contacts) is genuinely usable. Paid plans from $27/mo include unlimited email sends — ClickFunnels charges extra for that. We recommend it for anyone starting from $0.

Make.com

Not yet affiliated

No-code automation. The visual flow-chart builder is more powerful than Zapier for complex workflows. Free tier for learning, $9/mo Core plan to start. Worth the steeper learning curve if you want to sell automation services ($500-2,000 per project).

Beehiiv

Not yet affiliated

Best newsletter platform for new creators. Free up to 2,500 subscribers, built-in ad network once you hit 1,000. Built by ex-Morning Brew people. Downside: PayPal payouts only — confirm your country works before committing.

Kartra

Not yet affiliated

ClickFunnels' closest competitor. All-in-one for course creators and coaches. Has a 10% second-tier commission (rare). $99/mo Starter, $1 trial available. Often overlooked vs CF.

GetResponse

Not yet affiliated

Mature email marketing + automation + funnels platform (since 1998). 90-day cookie, tiered commissions, built-in webinar feature. Free up to 500 contacts, paid from $19/mo.

ElevenLabs

Not yet affiliated

Best-in-class AI voice generation. Use for YouTube narration, podcasts, audiobooks, course content. Free tier (10 min/mo), paid from $5/mo. Multilingual. 22% recurring commission if you become an affiliate partner.

Notion

Not yet affiliated

Workspace + sellable templates. Free for personal use. The 180-day cookie is the longest in the industry — slow-converting content still earns. Templates sell for $19-99 on Gumroad.

GoHighLevel

Not yet affiliated

All-in-one for marketing agencies. 40% lifetime recurring is the highest in the agency space. Steep learning curve but pays off if you're building an agency. White-label option available.

ActiveCampaign

Not yet affiliated

Email + CRM automation. Mature platform, 20-30% tiered commission. Strong for B2B and ecommerce. From $29/mo. Solid deliverability.

Kajabi

Not yet affiliated

Premium course/coaching platform. More polished UI than ClickFunnels. Higher price ($149-399/mo) but no transaction fees. Best for established course creators.

Teachable

Not yet affiliated

Standalone course platform. Simpler than ClickFunnels/Kajabi. From $39/mo (Basic) to $159/mo (Pro). Good if courses are your only product.

Thinkific

Not yet affiliated

Course platform alternative. Free plan available. From $49/mo. More flexibility than Teachable on payment options.

Surfer SEO

Not yet affiliated

AI content optimization for SEO. From $89/mo. Pairs with ChatGPT/Claude for ranking content. CPA is high ($111-274/sale) — one of the best commission rates in the SEO tool space.

Semrush

Not yet affiliated

Full SEO/marketing suite. $129.95-$449.95/mo. $200-300/sale CPA + $10/trial. The industry standard for serious SEO work.

Printful

Print-on-demand. Sell t-shirts, mugs, posters without inventory. ~10% commission on orders. Pairs with Etsy, Shopify, your own store. Good for creators who want physical products.

Shopify

Not yet affiliated

Ecommerce store builder. $25-150/merchant. Note for SEA: SEA merchants pay only $25 vs $150 for US/UK. Still useful if you're in a major market.

Coursera / Degree programs

Not yet affiliated

Up to 45% CPA on course enrollments. Good for content creators targeting career-upskillers. Less suited to pure "make money online" audiences.
