ClickFunnels — Honest Review

📅 Updated May 29, 2026 ⏱ 8 min read

ClickFunnels is the all-in-one sales funnel builder that pioneered the category. 10+ years in business, 100,000+ users, the largest "make money online" community on the web. If you've heard of any single funnel-building tool, it's probably this one. Here's the honest review.

Our Verdict

The default choice for funnel builders, for good reason.

ClickFunnels is more expensive than most alternatives and the templates are showing their age in some places. But the ecosystem, training, and community are unmatched. If you want a one-stop shop for funnels + email + courses + checkout, this is still the most proven option.

What it costs

Three tiers, all billed monthly or annually (annual = 2 months free):

  • Basic — $97/mo (or $81/mo annual): 20 funnels, 1 user, 5,000 contacts, 3 custom domains. Email is metered ($0.10/recipient above limits).
  • Pro — $197/mo (or $159/mo annual): Unlimited funnels + pages, 5 users, 25,000 contacts, 15 domains. Unlimited email sends.
  • Scale — $297/mo (or $247/mo annual): Everything unlimited, 10 users, priority support. Best for agencies and serious operators.

What we like

  • The ecosystem is unmatched. 10+ years of templates, training, community, and integrations. If you get stuck, there's a YouTube tutorial for it.
  • All-in-one that actually works. Funnels + email + courses + checkout + affiliate management — no need to stitch together 5 tools.
  • The training library is huge. Funnel Builder Secrets, DotComSecrets, Expert Secrets, Traffic Secrets, and the weekly webinars. If you consume all of it, you'll know more about funnels than 95% of marketers.
  • Pro and Scale plans have unlimited email. Not metered. If you have a big list, this saves real money.
  • The 14-day free trial requires no credit card. You can actually test it before committing.

What we don't like

  • Basic plan email is metered. $0.10 per recipient above 1,000 contacts. For an active list, this adds up fast.
  • Templates are showing their age. Some look like 2015-era internet marketing. You can build modern designs, but it takes more work.
  • Pricing is high vs alternatives. Systeme.io starts at $27/mo, Kartra at $99/mo. ClickFunnels is the premium option in the category.
  • Course hosting is locked to Scale plan ($297/mo). A big price jump for that single feature.
  • Affiliate management is basic. If you want real affiliate program software (tiered commissions, fraud detection, etc.), you need a separate tool like FirstPromoter or Tapfiliate.

Who should use ClickFunnels

  • You're selling a course, coaching program, or high-ticket service
  • You want one platform for funnels + email + checkout + courses
  • You value the training ecosystem (FBS, Traffic Secrets, etc.)
  • You can afford the $97-297/mo (or use the I'm All In bundle for 12mo of Scale for $1,970)

Who should look elsewhere

  • You're on a tight budget (try Systeme.io's free plan first)
  • You only need a simple landing page (try Carrd or Leadpages — much cheaper)
  • You already have a separate email tool and just need checkout (try Stripe + ThriveCart)
  • You don't sell anything yet — funnels are for sellers, not creators with no offer

The verdict

ClickFunnels is the default choice for a reason. Expensive compared to alternatives, but the ecosystem, training, and community are unmatched. For someone serious about building a funnel-based online business, it's still the most proven option.
