I'm All In — Funnel Builder Secrets
Russell Brunson's premium bundle

📅 Updated May 29, 2026 ⏱ 9 min read

"I'm All In" is the signature, premium upsell phrase used at the end of Russell Brunson's Funnel Builder Secrets (FBS) webinar. It's pitched to entrepreneurs who want the full ClickFunnels ecosystem — software, training, scripting, and a community — bundled into one purchase. The stated value is $9,349. The actual price: $1,970 one-time (typically with a payment-plan option too).

This is a review of what's actually inside the bundle, who it's for, and whether $1,970 is a real deal or a hard upsell. We bought it ourselves and went through every piece. Here's the honest take.

Our Verdict

A real value play for the right person. Not for beginners.

If you've already committed to ClickFunnels and want 12 months of the Scale plan plus Traffic Secrets training, $1,970 beats paying monthly for Scale + buying Traffic Secrets separately. If you're new to online business, the right first step is a free ClickFunnels trial — the bundle is for people who've already decided to go all-in.

What's actually in the bundle

Four pieces, each with a real standalone value:

1. 12 Months of ClickFunnels Scale

ClickFunnels Scale is the highest CF tier (~$2,487/yr if paid annually, or $297/mo monthly). It includes unlimited funnels, pages, contacts, follow-up emails, and the full feature set. Standalone value: ~$2,500/yr. Inside the bundle, you get 12 months at no extra cost — the savings are real if you'd otherwise pay for Scale monthly.

2. Traffic Secrets (book + course)

Russell Brunson's third book in the trilogy (after DotComSecrets and Expert Secrets), plus the full course version. The thesis: traffic is the lifeblood of any online business, and Brunson walks through 20+ traffic channels with real scripts. Standalone value: ~$200-500 depending on where you buy.

3. Traffic Swipe File

A pre-built collection of proven ad copy, headlines, and funnel scripts that you can deploy in your own funnels. Saves weeks of copywriting. Standalone value: ~$500-1,000 in time saved if you're a copywriter or $200-500 if you outsource.

4. Conversation Domination LIVE Community

Private community for traffic and funnel questions. Weekly Q&A, peer support, and access to Brunson's team. Useful if you learn well in groups, less useful if you'd rather figure it out alone. Standalone value: ~$200-500/yr (comparable private communities charge this).

Add those up and the math is roughly: $2,500 + $300 + $500 + $300 = $3,600. The bundle's stated value of $9,349 is inflated (classic webinar anchor-pricing), but the real value of $3,000-4,000 is still well above the $1,970 asking price. The savings are real if you'd buy all four anyway.

The honest pros

  • Real savings vs buying separately. If you'd pay for CF Scale + Traffic Secrets anyway, the bundle is a no-brainer — the math is in your favor.
  • 12 months of CF Scale is a long runway. You have a year to build funnels, learn the platform, and (hopefully) generate revenue before any renewal conversation.
  • The Traffic Secrets framework is genuinely useful. The "Dream 100" + channel-by-channel breakdown is a clear, repeatable process. Not magic, but a real system.
  • One ecosystem, one learning curve. Instead of stitching together ClickFunnels + a separate traffic course + a community, you have it all in one place.
  • 30-day refund window (verify at checkout). If the bundle isn't for you, you can exit cleanly within a month. Some high-ticket offers don't offer this.

The honest cons

  • Not for total beginners. If you haven't yet used ClickFunnels and aren't sure you want to build funnels for a living, $1,970 is a lot to spend before validating the platform. Start with a free trial first.
  • The $9,349 "value" is webinar anchor pricing. The actual combined value of the four pieces is closer to $3,000-4,000. The discount is still real, just not 79%.
  • ClickFunnels is a single platform. If your business model doesn't need funnels (e.g., a portfolio site, a marketplace, an app), this bundle doesn't make sense regardless of value.
  • 12 months of Scale is great, but the renewal is $2,487/yr. Plan for that. After year one, you'll need to decide if Scale is worth it — by then you should have data.
  • Brunson's teaching style is high-energy and sales-forward. Some people love the energy, some find it intense. Watch a free FBS webinar first to gauge fit before paying.

Who should buy it

  • You've already decided ClickFunnels is your platform of choice
  • You want a full year of the highest CF tier without paying monthly
  • You're actively trying to grow traffic and want the Traffic Secrets framework
  • You learn well in a community (or want access to one)
  • You have the capital to invest $1,970 without putting yourself in financial stress

Who should NOT buy it

  • You're brand new to online business and haven't yet validated the model
  • You're not sure ClickFunnels is the right platform for you (try the free trial first)
  • Your business doesn't need funnels (e.g., a service business without a sales page)
  • You'd be putting $1,970 on a credit card with no plan to pay it off
  • You want a low-risk, low-cost first step into online business — there are better starting points ($0-100 range)

The math, simplified

Buying the I'm All In bundle: $1,970 one-time. Includes 12mo CF Scale + Traffic Secrets + Swipe File + Community.

Buying separately over a year: $297/mo × 12 = $3,564 for CF Scale. Plus ~$300 for Traffic Secrets book + course. Total: ~$3,864.

Savings: ~$1,894 over the year, plus you keep the swipe file and community access.

That's the real comparison. If you'd pay for CF Scale monthly anyway, the bundle saves you roughly $1,900. If you wouldn't have paid monthly for Scale in the first place, the bundle is overpriced.

The verdict

If you've already decided on ClickFunnels: yes, it's a real deal. The savings vs paying monthly are clear, and Traffic Secrets is genuinely useful. If you're new to online business or undecided about ClickFunnels: no, start with the free trial first. The bundle is for people who've already validated the platform and want to commit. It's a premium purchase, not an entry point.
