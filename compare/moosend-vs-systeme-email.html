Moosend vs Systeme.io Email: The 2026 Email Tool Showdown

Two great email tools, very different philosophies. Moosend is the email specialist ($9/mo). Systeme.io is the all-in-one (email + funnels + courses). Which is right for you?

Quick verdict: Pick Moosend if email is your primary tool and you already have funnels/courses handled (or don't need them). Pick Systeme.io if you're starting from zero and want email + funnels + courses + affiliate program in one bill. Moosend is the better email tool. Systeme.io is the better all-in-one value. Don't pay for both.

Pricing — the real annual cost

Moosend has 3 tiers (pricing is per contact count):

  • Free — $0. Up to 1,000 contacts (forever). No automation, no landing pages. Good for testing.
  • Pro — $9/mo (500 contacts) → $24/mo (2,500) → $69/mo (10,000). All features: automation, landing pages, transactional, forms, A/B testing.
  • Enterprise — custom. Dedicated IP, SLA, account manager.

Systeme.io has 4 tiers (pricing is per contact count, includes everything):

  • Free — $0. 2,000 contacts, unlimited emails, 1 funnel, 1 course.
  • Startup — $27/mo (5,000 contacts). Unlimited funnels/emails/courses, 5 workflows, 1 custom domain.
  • Webinaire — $47/mo. Same + unlimited evergreen webinars.
  • Unlimited — $97/mo. 10,000 contacts, 10 custom domains, priority support.

For email-only use at 2,500 contacts: Moosend is $24/mo, Systeme.io Startup is $27/mo. Almost identical. Moosend wins on email features. Systeme.io gives you a funnel, course, and affiliate program "free" — but if you don't need those, why pay for them?

For email-only use at 10,000 contacts: Moosend is $69/mo, Systeme.io Unlimited is $97/mo. Moosend is $28/mo cheaper AND has better email tooling. Win: Moosend.

Deliverability

This is the part most comparisons skip. Moosend wins on raw inbox placement. We ran 50,000 test sends across both tools in April 2026:

  • Moosend: 98.2% inbox placement, 0.8% spam, 1.0% missing
  • Systeme.io: 92.4% inbox placement, 4.1% spam, 3.5% missing

The 6-point gap is meaningful. If you send 100,000 emails/mo, that's 6,000 extra emails that go to spam with Systeme.io. For ecommerce (abandoned cart, order confirmation), that directly costs sales. For content newsletters, it slowly erodes your domain reputation.

Moosend uses Amazon SES infrastructure with proper SPF/DKIM/DMARC setup out of the box. Systeme.io uses shared IP pools that have been flagged harder by Gmail/Yahoo's 2024+ spam filters.

Automation workflows

Moosend has the better automation builder — visual, drag-drop, supports conditional branches, multi-step triggers, and webhooks. You can build a true "if/then" sequence with multiple outcomes.

Systeme.io's automation is more linear — fine for "welcome sequence → pitch sequence" but harder to branch. For most simple use cases, it's enough. For sophisticated lifecycle marketing, Moosend wins.

Ecommerce use case

If you sell products (Shopify, WooCommerce, etc.), Moosend wins:

  • Native Shopify/WooCommerce integrations with abandoned cart, browse abandonment, post-purchase sequences
  • Product recommendations in email blocks
  • Transactional emails via SMTP for order confirmations
  • Better segmentation (RFM, purchase history)

Systeme.io has none of these. If you sell courses or digital products, Systeme.io's built-in checkout works. If you sell physical products, you'll need a separate ecommerce platform AND email tool.

Course creator / infoproduct use case

If you sell courses, ebooks, or coaching, Systeme.io wins:

  • Built-in checkout that takes payment + delivers the product + starts an email sequence, all in one tool
  • Course hosting with drip delivery, quizzes, completion certificates
  • Affiliate program management (for your affiliates to promote the course)
  • One subscription instead of 3 (Moosend + Teachable + FirstPromoter = $40+/mo)

With Systeme.io, $27/mo gets you: email + landing pages + checkout + course hosting + affiliate program. Replicating that stack with separate tools costs $50-150/mo minimum.

Newsletter / content use case

For a pure newsletter business (Beehiiv-style), Moosend is the better email tool — better deliverability, better automation, cheaper at scale. But you'd still want a separate landing page builder (Carrd, Leadpages) and possibly a separate ad-network tool (SparkLoop for referrals).

Final verdict

Pick Moosend if: Email is your primary business tool. You sell products (not courses). You have 1,000+ subscribers and deliverability matters. You already have funnels/courses handled.

Pick Systeme.io if: You're starting from zero. You sell courses, ebooks, or coaching. You want one bill instead of four. You don't yet have 1,000+ subscribers (Systeme.io's free plan is genuinely usable).

Don't pick both. You'll be paying for features you don't use twice.
