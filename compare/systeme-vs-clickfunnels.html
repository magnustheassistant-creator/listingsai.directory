Systeme.io — our #1 pick for new businesses

60% recurring for life is the highest sustainable commission rate in this space. Systeme.io is also the only one of the two with a free plan that's actually usable (2,000 contacts, full funnels, no credit card).

Where Systeme.io wins:

  • Free plan is real. 2,000 contacts + email + funnels + 1 course. Enough to validate a business idea before paying a cent.
  • Email is included unlimited. Not metered, not $0.10 per send like ClickFunnels. This alone is worth switching for many users.
  • 60% vs 40%. A $97/mo ClickFunnels customer pays you $38.80/mo. A $97/mo Systeme.io customer pays you $58.20/mo. That's 50% more recurring revenue per referral.
  • Modern, clean templates. Less "old-school internet marketing" vibe than ClickFunnels.

Where Systeme.io falls short:

  • Email deliverability is OK, not best-in-class. If you're sending 100k+ emails/mo, you'd want a dedicated ESP.
  • Template variety is smaller. ~30 templates vs 100+ for ClickFunnels. Quality over quantity, but fewer options.
  • Less community/affiliate mindshare. ClickFunnels has a much bigger "how to make money" ecosystem. Systeme.io is catching up.

ClickFunnels — the established leader

ClickFunnels has been the dominant funnel builder for 10+ years. It pioneered the "all-in-one" model and has the largest user community. If your audience already knows and trusts ClickFunnels, that's worth something.

Where ClickFunnels wins:

  • Brand recognition. Most "make money online" marketers have heard of ClickFunnels. Easier to convert warm traffic.
  • Larger template library. 100+ funnel templates for every niche imaginable.
  • More integrations. Native connections to more third-party tools.
  • Better training ecosystem. Tons of YouTube tutorials and courses teaching ClickFunnels specifically.

Where ClickFunnels falls short:

  • No real free plan. 14-day trial requires a credit card upfront.
  • Email is metered on the basic plan. $0.10 per recipient above 1,000 contacts adds up fast.
  • Course hosting is locked to the $297/mo Premium plan. A massive price jump for that feature.
  • Lower commission (40% vs 60%). Same effort, less recurring revenue for you.

The verdict

For new online businesses and anyone starting fresh: Systeme.io. The 60% commission is unbeatable, the free plan is real, and email/courses are included without hidden fees. ClickFunnels still wins if your audience is full of experienced marketers who already trust the brand — but for a new affiliate, the math is much better with Systeme.io.

If you're choosing which to recommend to your audience: Systeme.io. Lower monthly cost for the user, better free plan, and the economics work for both of you. The only reason to recommend ClickFunnels over Systeme.io is brand recognition or a specific feature ClickFunnels has that Systeme.io doesn't.
