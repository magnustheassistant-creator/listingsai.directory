Systeme.io vs ClickFunnels: The 2026 Honest Comparison

Systeme.io is free (or $27/mo). ClickFunnels is $127-297/mo. Is ClickFunnels 10× better? We use both. Here's the real answer.

Quick verdict: Pick Systeme.io if you're starting out, on a budget, or only need one or two of the features (funnels + email, mostly). Pick ClickFunnels if you're already making money and the speed/ecosystem/community of CF is worth the premium. They're not the same product at different prices — they target genuinely different users.

Pricing deep-dive

Systeme.io has four tiers:

  • Free — $0. 2,000 contacts, unlimited emails, 1 funnel, 1 course, 1 blog. Real free plan with no credit card. Good for testing the waters.
  • Startup — $27/mo annual ($30.68/mo monthly). 5,000 contacts, unlimited funnels/emails/courses, 5 workflows, 1 custom domain, A/B testing.
  • Webinaire — $47/mo annual. Same as Startup + unlimited webinars (Systeme is one of the few all-in-ones with built-in evergreen webinars).
  • Unlimited — $97/mo annual. 10,000 contacts, everything unlimited, 10 custom domains, priority support.

ClickFunnels has three tiers (as of June 2026):

  • Basic — $127/mo (was $99 historically). 20 funnels, 1 user, 1 custom domain, basic email (no automation).
  • Pro — $197/mo. Unlimited funnels/pages, 5 users, 3 domains, Follow-Up Funnels (automation), priority support.
  • Scale — $297/mo. Unlimited everything + 10 users + dedicated CSM + advanced reporting.

The math: Systeme.io Startup is $324/yr. ClickFunnels Pro is $2,364/yr. The "savings" of going with Systeme.io for 5 years is $10,200 — enough to buy a car or a serious ad budget. Whether that $10K is worth the polish/features gap depends on what you're doing (next sections).

The catch with ClickFunnels pricing: they discount heavily if you pay annually, and the I'm All In bundle (covered below) gets you 12 months of Scale plan for $1,970 one-time — which is a 45% discount.

Feature-by-feature

Funnel builder

ClickFunnels wins on funnel-building UX, full stop. The drag-and-drop editor is faster, the templates are better, the one-click upsell/downsell/order-bump flow is industry-leading. You can build a 5-step funnel in 2 hours. With Systeme.io, the same funnel takes 4-6 hours, and some advanced funnel types (like a true multi-product order form with multiple bumps) require creative workarounds.

If funnels are your business, ClickFunnels is worth the price for this reason alone.

Email marketing

Closer than you'd think. Both have automation, both have templates, both have decent deliverability. ClickFunnels' Follow-Up Funnels is more visual and easier to build complex sequences with. Systeme.io's email is more straightforward — fewer features, but the basics are solid. For pure email, we use Moosend ($9/mo for 500 contacts) on top of either of these.

Course hosting

Both work. ClickFunnels' course player is prettier, but Systeme.io's is more functional (better quiz support, drip delivery, completion certificates). If courses are 50%+ of your business, ClickFunnels. If courses are a side feature, Systeme.io is fine.

Affiliate program management

Systeme.io wins here — it has a real built-in affiliate program with commissions, links, and tracking. ClickFunnels doesn't; you need a third-party tool like FirstPromoter or Rewardful ($50-200/mo extra). For affiliate-driven businesses, Systeme.io saves you $600-2,400/yr on top of platform cost.

Webinars

Systeme.io has built-in evergreen webinars. ClickFunnels doesn't — you'd use a separate tool (EverWebinar, Demio, WebinarJam). If webinars are a key part of your funnel, this matters.

Templates

ClickFunnels has 200+ tested funnel templates (most famously the "DotComSecrets" and "ExpertSecrets" templates). Systeme.io has ~30. If you want to copy a proven funnel structure verbatim, ClickFunnels wins.

Community & training

ClickFunnels wins with FunnelFlix (training library), FunnelScripts (copywriting AI), CFHolics (Facebook group with 200K+ members), and the regular "Funnel Hacking Live" events. The ecosystem is the real moat. Systeme.io has a smaller, more casual Facebook group.

Pick Systeme.io if…

  • You're starting from $0 and want to test the funnel-builder model before committing
  • You need email + funnels + courses + affiliate program in one tool, and don't want to pay for 4 subscriptions
  • You sell an info product (course, ebook) and need to host + deliver + take payment + email customers
  • You're running webinars (Systeme.io's built-in evergreen webinar is rare)
  • You want to bootstrap a business on a $27/mo budget, not a $297/mo one

Pick ClickFunnels if…

  • You're already making $5K+/mo and the speed of iteration pays for itself in days
  • You sell physical products with order bumps / upsells / downsells and need the proven funnel types
  • You want to copy a Russell Brunson / CF ecosystem funnel structure
  • You want the community + training (FunnelFlix alone is worth $1K/yr)
  • You need a real CRM with segmentation, scoring, and pipeline management

Final verdict

For 80% of new builders: Systeme.io. The free plan lets you test, the $27/mo Startup plan is the best value in the all-in-one category, and you can always graduate to ClickFunnels later when revenue justifies it.

For 20% of established marketers: ClickFunnels, ideally through the IAI bundle if you can commit to 12 months. The polish and ecosystem pay for themselves when time = money.
