How does ListingsAI make money?

We earn affiliate commissions when you sign up for tools through our links. It costs you nothing extra — the company pays us a referral fee. We only recommend things we'd use ourselves. Read our full affiliate disclosure.

Why should I trust your reviews?

We test tools ourselves before recommending them. We don't accept payment for reviews. If something is bad, we say so. Read our editorial methodology.

Is it really free to start with these tools?

Most tools we recommend have a free plan or free trial. When we say "free to start" we mean it — no credit card required in most cases. We flag the ones that aren't actually free.

I'm not in the US. Will these tools work for me?

Many of the tools we list are global or have solid international support. We flag US-only or region-limited programs in every review with a "Works globally?" note. We test payout methods for SEA, Latin America, and other underserved regions specifically.
