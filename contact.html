What to email us about

Spotted an inaccuracy?

Wrong pricing, broken link, outdated info, missing "works globally" note? Tell us the page and the issue — we fix these fast.

Want us to review a tool?

Send us the tool name, what it does, and why you think it fits. We review every suggestion but can't promise coverage — we only list things we'd recommend.

Partnership or press inquiry?

If you run a tool, network, or media outlet and want to talk, send a short note. We don't do paid placements, but we do consider legit collaborations.

Affiliate program manager?

If you run an affiliate program and want us to consider your tool, send a one-pager with the program terms, commission rate, and a brief on what the tool does. We'll review and reply.

What we don't reply to

To save everyone's time:

  • SEO link-exchange requests ("I'll link to you if you link to me")
  • Guest post pitches for irrelevant niches
  • Generic "collaborate with us" outreach from agencies
  • Crypto, NFT, or trading-platform pitches
