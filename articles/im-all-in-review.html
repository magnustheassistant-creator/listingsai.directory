I'm All In (Funnel Builder Secrets Bundle) Review: Is the $1,970 One-Time Payment Worth It?

The I'm All In bundle promises 12 months of ClickFunnels Scale, the Traffic Secrets course, and a private community for $1,970 instead of $3,564. Here's the math, the catch, and who should buy it.

Quick verdict: The IAI bundle is one of the few ClickFunnels offers that genuinely saves you money — if you're committed to using ClickFunnels for 12+ months. If you're on the fence, this review will tell you whether to commit.

What is I'm All In (IAI)?

IAI is the rebrand of Russell Brunson's old "Funnel Builder Secrets" (FBS) bundle. It's a one-time $1,970 payment that gives you:

  1. 12 months of ClickFunnels Scale (their top-tier plan) — retail value $297/mo × 12 = $3,564
  2. The Traffic Secrets course (Russell Brunson's $1,997 course on traffic generation)
  3. Access to the private "One Funnel Away" community for 12 months
  4. Several bonus courses (Copywriting, Hooks, etc. — minor value)

Stack-rank the value: the ClickFunnels Scale plan alone is $3,564 if paid monthly. The Traffic Secrets course is genuinely good and retails at $1,997. The community access is worth $500-1,000/year depending on your stage.

Total real value: $5,500-6,500. You pay $1,970. The math works out to roughly a 60-70% discount.

The pricing math (and the catch)

The headline is appealing. The catch is the renewal price.

After the 12 months are up, ClickFunnels Scale will renew at the standard $297/mo. There's no continued bundle discount. If you want to keep using ClickFunnels Scale after 12 months, you pay full price.

There's also a 30-day money-back guarantee, which is real (we know multiple people who used it). If you decide within 30 days that it's not for you, you can request a refund.

What's actually included

1. ClickFunnels Scale (12 months)

The full ClickFunnels Scale tier for 12 months. Same features as paying $297/mo: unlimited funnels, unlimited pages, 9 users, priority support, weekly group coaching calls with CF experts, and access to CF University (the training library).

This is the bulk of the value. If you were going to pay $297/mo for ClickFunnels anyway, the bundle pays for itself in 7 months.

2. Traffic Secrets course

Russell's flagship course on traffic generation. It's not new (released 2020) but the principles are still solid: pick a "traffic temperature" (cold/warm/hot), build a "warm hive" of followers, and run paid traffic to a funnel.

The course is 6+ hours of video, 50+ modules, with templates and swipe files. It's a real course, not a 90-minute teaser.

3. One Funnel Away (OFA) Challenge community

The private community attached to the OFA 30-day challenge. Members are mostly new and intermediate ClickFunnels users. The community is active on Facebook. Value: depends on your stage. If you're starting out, the peer support is helpful. If you're experienced, you'll outgrow it in 2-3 months.

4. Bonus courses (Hooks, Copywriting, etc.)

These are short courses (3-8 hours each) by various CF affiliates. Quality varies. The Hooks course is genuinely useful. The Copywriting course is a Russel Brunson remix of his book. Skip the rest.

Who should buy the bundle

You should buy the IAI bundle if:

  • You've decided to commit to ClickFunnels for 12+ months (the math works)
  • You want Traffic Secrets anyway (it's the only funnel-traffic course that teaches the "warm hive" strategy in depth)
  • You learn better in a community than solo
  • You have $1,970 to spend on tools without straining cash flow

Who should skip the bundle

Skip the bundle if:

  • You're not sure you'll use ClickFunnels for 12+ months
  • You're pre-revenue (use the 14-day ClickFunnels free trial first, then decide)
  • You already own Traffic Secrets (you can buy the ClickFunnels piece separately through a different offer)
  • You have a hot deal on the $127/mo Starter plan (sometimes CF runs $81/mo promos — the math might not justify the bundle for the lower tier)

Alternatives to consider

  • ClickFunnels monthly ($127-297/mo) — no commitment, cancel anytime. Best for: trying ClickFunnels first.
  • ClickFunnels 14-day free trial — no credit card. Best for: building one funnel to decide.
  • Systeme.io free plan — covers 70% of what CF does for $0. Best for: budget-conscious.
  • Just buy Traffic Secrets separately — if you want the course but not the ClickFunnels commitment. Sometimes available for $497 standalone on Russ's site.

Final verdict

The IAI bundle is one of the few high-ticket offers in the ClickFunnels ecosystem that delivers on its promise. If you've decided ClickFunnels is your tool of choice, this is the cheapest way in for the first 12 months.

If you haven't decided: start with the 14-day free trial. Build one funnel. If you make more than $200 in the trial, the bundle is a no-brainer. If you don't, ClickFunnels isn't the right tool, and the bundle won't change that.
