Dealify Review: A Curated AppSumo Alternative Worth Knowing

Dealify is a lifetime-deal marketplace with a smaller, more curated catalog than AppSumo. After 8 months of use, here's the honest take on who should use it (and who should stick with AppSumo).

Quick verdict: Dealify is what AppSumo was in 2014 — a small, curated catalog of legitimate lifetime deals on real software. The curation is genuinely better. The catalog is smaller. If you know what you want, Dealify is the better experience.

What is Dealify?

Dealify is a lifetime-deal marketplace that launched in 2018. It works like AppSumo: software makers list their tools at a one-time price (lifetime access) instead of a monthly subscription, and Dealify takes a cut.

The key difference: Dealify is much more selective about which tools get listed. They manually review each submission. The result is a smaller catalog (200-300 active deals vs. AppSumo's 1,000+) but a higher average quality.

The catalog reality

At any given time, Dealify has 200-300 active deals across these categories:

  • Marketing & SEO tools (around 30%)
  • Productivity & project management (~20%)
  • Design & creative tools (~15%)
  • AI tools (chatbots, image generators, copywriters — ~15%)
  • CRM, sales, e-commerce (~10%)
  • Everything else (~10%)

For comparison, AppSumo has 1,000+ active deals in similar categories, plus a bigger selection of courses and digital products.

The pricing model

Dealify deals are typically priced at 30-70% off the equivalent subscription plan. Some popular examples from the catalog:

  • Pixelied (design tool) — $49 lifetime (vs. $96/yr subscription)
  • FlowFAQ (FAQ tool) — $69 lifetime (vs. $228/yr)
  • Briefcase (legal contracts) — $79 lifetime (vs. $348/yr)
  • Jupitrr (AI video editor) — $59 lifetime (vs. $180/yr)

The math works: most deals pay for themselves in 6-12 months of equivalent subscription cost.

Pros and cons

What we liked

  • ★★★★★ Curation quality. Most deals on Dealify are real, working tools with real companies behind them. The 2018-era AppSumo "spam" problem is rare here.
  • ★★★★★ Refund policy. 60-day refund window, no questions asked. This is more generous than AppSumo's 60-day (which is now 30-day for newer deals).
  • ★★★★☆ Deal pages. Each deal has a thorough description, screenshots, video demo, user reviews, and FAQs. Better than AppSumo's often-thin deal pages.
  • ★★★★☆ Less FOMO. Dealify runs 1-2 major sales a year (Black Friday, anniversary). The catalog doesn't rotate every 6 hours like AppSumo, so you can take time to evaluate.

What we didn't like

  • ★★☆☆☆ Smaller catalog. If you need a specific tool, it might not be on Dealify. AppSumo's massive catalog is still the place to find obscure tools.
  • ★★★☆☆ Fewer "hero" deals. AppSumo has a track record of breaking new tools (the "AppSumo launch" is a milestone for SaaS founders). Dealify's deals are more established tools, less "hidden gem" energy.
  • ★★★☆☆ No community reviews as deep. AppSumo has a public Q&A and review system. Dealify's review system is more limited.

Who should use Dealify

  • Founders, indie hackers, and small teams looking for productivity tools at lifetime prices
  • People who got burned by AppSumo's lower curation and want a safer alternative
  • Anyone who knows what specific tool category they want (e.g., "I need a design tool")
  • People who want a smaller, less overwhelming catalog

Who should use AppSumo instead

  • Deal hunters who want to discover new tools
  • People looking for courses, ebooks, or templates (AppSumo has way more)
  • Anyone who needs a specific tool that's only on AppSumo
  • People who like the "daily deal" experience

Final verdict

Dealify is what AppSumo should be. The curation is real, the deals are legitimate, and the refund policy is generous. The smaller catalog is the only real downside.

For most people, we'd recommend checking Dealify first for any specific tool category you're shopping for, and falling back to AppSumo if Dealify doesn't have what you need.
