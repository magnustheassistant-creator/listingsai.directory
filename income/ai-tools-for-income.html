HubSpot — High-Ticket Enterprise

HubSpot is the big dog in inbound marketing and CRM. But with plans starting at $800/month, it's not for everyone. Only promote to B2B audiences with budget.

  • 30% recurring for first 12 months of customer
  • 180-day cookie — long tracking window
  • Strong brand authority — higher conversions for B2B
  • Higher ticket = higher commission but harder to get signups

Best for: Affiliates targeting B2B, marketing agencies, SaaS reviewers.

Which Should You Promote?

For most affiliates: ClickFunnels is the clear winner. Highest commission (40%), well-known brand, accessible price point, generous cookie, and conversion-optimized for affiliates. Promote ClickFunnels first. Add HubSpot if your audience is B2B.
