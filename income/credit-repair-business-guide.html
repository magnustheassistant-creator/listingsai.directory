What is Credit Repair?

Credit repair is the process of helping consumers remove inaccurate negative items from their credit reports. This includes late payments, collections, charge-offs, bankruptcies, and more. By law, credit bureaus must remove inaccurate items within 30 days of dispute.

As an affiliate, you connect people with credit repair services. The service handles the work. You earn a commission for each person who signs up through your referral.

How Credit Repair Affiliates Work

  • Per Lead: $50-$200 when someone submits their contact info
  • Per Sale: $100-$500 when someone pays for credit repair service
  • Recurring: Some programs pay monthly as long as customer stays active
