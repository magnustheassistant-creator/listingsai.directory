Penn Foster HVAC

Penn Foster is one of the most widely promoted HVAC programs by affiliates. Self-paced online learning with an optional HVAC kit for hands-on practice. Accredited by DEAC.

  • Programs include HVAC/R technician certificate and EPA 608 exam prep
  • Payment plans available — tuition can be spread over 12-18 months
  • Career services included: resume help, interview prep, job search guidance
  • Affiliate program: $200-500 per enrolled student

Best for: Career changers who need flexibility and can't attend campus-based programs.

Ashworth College HVAC

Ashworth College is similar to Penn Foster — online, self-paced, affordable. Also accredited by DEAC and offers bundled programs for HVAC + refrigeration.

  • Lower tuition than Penn Foster — often the budget pick
  • VA-approved — works for veterans using education benefits
  • Bundled programs: HVAC + refrigeration technician package available
  • Affiliate program: $200-500 per enrolled student

Best for: Veterans and those looking for the most affordable option with payment plans.

UTI (Universal Technical Institute)

UTI is the campus-based option with manufacturer partnerships (e.g., Snap-on, Lincoln Electric). Higher tuition but stronger job placement through employer relationships.

  • Campus-based with hands-on labs (not fully online)
  • Manufacturer partnerships mean employer recruitment on campus
  • Programs: HVAC/R, automotive, diesel — broader career options
  • Higher tuition reflects campus infrastructure and employer relationships

Best for: Those who prefer hands-on learning and can commit to campus attendance.

Which Should You Promote as an Affiliate?

Penn Foster and Ashworth have the strongest affiliate programs with established commission structures. UTI has higher tuition which makes conversions harder. Our recommendation for affiliates: promote Penn Foster and Ashworth. Both have payment plans making them accessible, and both pay $200-500 per enrollment.
