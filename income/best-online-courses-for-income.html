How to Choose a Course to Promote

  • High conversion rate: Well-known brand, proven sales funnel, good reviews
  • Fair affiliate terms: 30%+ commission, 30+ day cookie, reliable tracking
  • Quality product: Students get results, testimonials are real
  • Evergreen or regular launches: Income doesn't dry up between launches
  • Recurring or upsell potential: Monthly membership or upsells mean more commissions

Top Course Niches for Affiliates

Business & Entrepreneurship

Highest commissions. Courses on starting a business, marketing, sales. $500-$5,000 price points common.

Trading & Investing

Stocks, crypto, real estate investing courses. High-ticket but need financial disclaimers.

Skills & Trades

HVAC, plumbing, welding, electrical. More stable, lower refunds.

Where to Find Course Affiliate Programs

  • Affiliate networks: ShareASale, CJ Affiliate, Impact — search "course" or "education"
  • Direct programs: Most course creators have affiliate pages. Search "[course name] affiliate"
  • Course platforms: Teachable, Kajabi, Thinkific all have affiliate programs
